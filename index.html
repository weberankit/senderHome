Welcome to the SENDER

Our extension helps job seekers send emails quickly by allowing them to use saved templates. With just one click, users can send customized emails directly through Gmail, saving valuable time during their job search process.

To enable this functionality, the extension requires Gmail authorization to send emails on your behalf.

Why Gmail Authorization?
The Gmail API allows us to send pre-saved messages directly from your Gmail account, automating the email process for job applications.
